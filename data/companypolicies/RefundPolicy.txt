Refund Policy

At EcoMart, we strive to ensure that every customer is satisfied with their purchase. If for any reason you are not completely happy with your order, we offer a straightforward refund policy to provide you with peace of mind.

1. Eligibility for Refunds:

Refunds are available for items that meet the following criteria:
The item is returned within 30 days of delivery.
The item is in its original condition, unused, and with all original tags and packaging intact.
The item is not a final sale or non-returnable item (as specified at the time of purchase).
2. Initiation of Refund:

To initiate a refund, please contact our customer service team at support@ecomart.com or call +123456789 within the eligible timeframe.
Provide your order number and a brief explanation of the reason for the refund request.
Our customer service team will guide you through the return process and provide you with a return authorization if applicable.
3. Return Shipping:

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a mistake on our part (e.g., wrong item shipped, item arrived damaged).
We recommend using a trackable shipping method to ensure the safe return of the item.
4. Refund Processing:

Once we receive the returned item and verify its eligibility for a refund, we will process the refund within 5-7 business days.
Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
5. Refund Exceptions:

Certain items may not be eligible for refunds, including:
Personalized or customized items.
Perishable goods.
Gift cards or vouchers.
Final sale items or items marked as non-returnable at the time of purchase are not eligible for refunds unless they arrive damaged or defective.
6. Damaged or Defective Items:

If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact us immediately upon receipt.
Provide photographic evidence of the damage or defect for our records.
We will arrange for a replacement or issue a full refund, including return shipping costs if applicable.
7. Exchanges:

We currently do not offer exchanges for items. If you wish to exchange an item for a different size, color, or style, please initiate a return and place a new order for the desired item.
8. Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about our refund policy, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team at support@ecomart.com or call +123456789. We are here to assist you and ensure a positive shopping experience.
Note: This refund policy is subject to change at the discretion of EcoMart. Please refer to our website or contact customer service for the most up-to-date information.
